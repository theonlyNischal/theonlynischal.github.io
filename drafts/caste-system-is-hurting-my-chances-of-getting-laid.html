This is why the caste system in Nepal is statistically hurting my chances of getting laid.

By Nischal Lal Shrestha | On Tue, Aug 29 2023 | Filed under Thoughts

Being an obedient boy from a Newar family, I have always been told to marry only within my caste. However, upon reviewing the latest census data from 2023, I don't believe I have a very high hope of getting married.

Raised to marry in my caste, but the 2023 census just played matchmaker with my expectations.

Nischal

The total Newar female population in Nepal stands at 685,219. Among them, only 5.1% fall within the age range of 20-24, which means the potential pool of candidates for me is 34,947.

685,219 Newar females, but only 34,947 in the 'right age' bracket? It's like searching for a WiFi signal in a desert!

However, I find myself contending with Newar males within the age range of 20-34, which accounts for 11.7% of the newar population, or 156,940 individuals. Yet, not all of them may be single, especially those aged above 24. Let's assume that 100% of men between the ages of 20-24 are single, along with 50% of those aged 24-30, and 20% of those aged 30-34. Consequently, I would be vying for attention among 7.08% of Newar males, equating to 94,969 individuals.

685,219 Newar females, but only 34,947 in the 'right age' bracket? It's like searching for a WiFi signal in a desert!

However, that's not all. There are also 7.08% of eligible men from other castes to consider. Let's assume that 2% of these men choose to marry a Newar girl. This translates to 0.0708 * 0.02 * Total Male population, amounting to 18,284.

In this scenario, the effective competition pool encompasses both Newar males (94,969) and marriageable males from other castes (18,284), totaling 113,253 individuals. Consequently, I am faced with the task of vying for attention among this total pool of males, while seeking to establish connections with the relatively smaller group of 34,947 eligible Newar females.

But hey, with a 30% chance of success, at least my romantic GPS isn't completely lost!

Odds of Getting laid = Total Eligible Newar Females / Total Competing Males Odds = 34,947 / 113,253 ≈ 0.308, or about 30.8%
